Research Interests

I am interested in statistical physics, both in and out of equilibrium, as well as soft matter. My research primarily focuses on emergent collective phenomena in systems composed of particles that follow non-equilibrium dynamics, such as active particles. Since my primary research tool is numerical simulations, I am also interested in the development of algorithms.
Some key words summarizing my interests:
non-equilibrium statistical physics, soft matter, active matter, dense systems, numerical simulations, geometrical phase transitions, disordered systems, two-dimensional melting, development of algorithms
